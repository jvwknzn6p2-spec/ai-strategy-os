# Genesis Principles

The founding principles of AI Strategy OS.

## 1. Expansion, Not Replacement

AI is not used to replace human thought.
AI is used to expand human thought.

## 2. Human-AI Partnership

Humans and AI think together: reviewing ideas, generating strategies,
and transforming information into useful contribution.

## 3. Verification Over Belief

Every prediction, model, and strategy must be testable.
Backtesting and measurement come before trust.

## 4. Transparency

Reasoning, assumptions, and data sources are documented so that
decisions can be understood and challenged.

## 5. Iteration

Start small, ship something usable, learn, and improve.
Each module begins as an experiment before becoming infrastructure.
